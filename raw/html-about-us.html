About Us

The owners of SDE-Tech have over 20 years experience working in the Information Technology arena. They have experience working for large companies with over 1000 employees to small offices of 2 to 5 people and everything in between. There’s rarely any type of situation they have not seen or problem they have not solved. Experience really counts when the clock is ticking and it’s on your dime. While most shops “nickel and dime” you by stretching out the solutions, we pride ourselves on getting in and out quickly, but without cutting corners so you can focus on your bottom line. We have straight, easy-to-calculate rates which are below market most of the time. We have built a following of clients on quality, reputation, and honest pricing.

SDE-Tech had its start by maintaining a large number of medical offices. These offices always have the latest in technology when it comes to computers, Internet connectivity, phone systems, and specialized business applications. Our employees have worked in all types of businesses from manufacturing, retail, wholesale, shipping/packaging, web-based companies, healthcare, financial and others.
